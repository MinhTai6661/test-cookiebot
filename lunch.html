Lunch

If you're craving a delicious ramen lunch in Amsterdam,
come to the authentic Japanese ramen spot “RAMEN-ISM”.

A flavor found
only at ISM.

Exclusive
Lunch Set.

Japanese dishes
alongside ramen.

We're conveniently located just a short walk
from Amsterdam Central Station
and the picturesque Lange Niezel area
—perfect for popping in during a day of sightseeing or a busy work break.
Our top recommendation is the special lunch set available only from 12:00-15:00.

Choose your favorite ramen
—which we are constantly evolving to be even more delicious
—add either gyoza or takoyaki,
plus one drink, all at a great value.

Many sides are vegan-friendly too,
so you can easily enjoy a taste of Japanese soul food,
whether you're vegetarian, vegan, or just curious to try something new.

GROUP BOOKINGS

We have 9 tables on the 1st floor and 8 on the 2nd floor.
The 1st floor can seat up to 17 guests, and the 2nd floor up to 14
—ideal for group travel, sightseeing tours, or casual get-togethers.
Please feel free to reach out; we’re happy to help arrange a great meal for your group.

We also welcome party-style visits.
Sip your favorite drinks while enjoying Japanese dishes
and sharing good times with friends or loved ones.

*For groups of 7 or more, we handle bookings via request only—please send us a request, and we’ll confirm availability
and finalize your reservation once everything is set.
