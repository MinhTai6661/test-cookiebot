"We want you to feel the true ISM—the heart and soul of Japan—not just in the ramen itself, but in every detail: the cozy space, the thoughtful service and the warmth of hospitality." With this passion in mind, we brought a chef straight from Japan and opened "RAMEN-ISM" in the heart of Amsterdam in 2024.

Our "ISM series" ramen is crafted with unwavering dedication—no compromises—drawing on years of experience and genuine love for ramen back home in Japan, while embracing the unique local inspiration we find here in Netherlands.

House-made noodles,
tailored to every bowl.

Every noodle we serve is handmade fresh every morning at our noodle workshop. Using traditional techniques, we create our signature chewy texture with a specialized hydration and flour mixture ratio.

Instead of offering a fixed selection, we craft the specific noodle type to perfectly complement each unique ramen broth. It's one of the little details we're most proud of.

Soups,
refined again
and again.

In Japanese ramen, the broth (dashi) is everything—
it's the soul of the dish.

After countless refinements,
we created our signature "ISM series" broth by delicately layering rich umami from chicken, pork, beef and seafood.

A perfect example of our philosophy is the EBI-ISM.

Driven by the Japanese "Mottainai spirit"
—a deep respect for resources and the desire to let nothing go to waste
—we utilize every part of the shrimp and vegetables
to extract their maximum flavor and aroma.

By treating each ingredient with this level of care,
we create a soup so aromatic and deeply savory
that it captures the essence of our dedication in every drop.

ISM Original Menu

Our Plant-Based Ramen, supervised by the MEN' IMPOSSIBLE brand, draws its rich umami from a variety of vegetables and Japanese shiitake mushrooms, offering a hearty satisfaction that surprises even non-vegans.

We put just as much love into our side dishes as we do into the ramen.
You'll find classic Japanese favorites like gyoza, edamame, and karaage, plus plenty of other tempting options.
We use authentic Japanese seasonings in our sauces,
but we've tuned the flavors to feel approachable and enjoyable even for first-timers in Amsterdam.

We also offer a wide selection of homemade drinks that pair perfectly with our ramen—so whether you're joining us for lunch or dinner, we invite you to relax and enjoy your time with us.
