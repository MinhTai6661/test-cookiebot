ABOUT

Why not experience authentic Japanese ramen
right in the heart of Amsterdam, Netherlands?

"RAMEN-ISM" lives up to its name:
it's a special Japanese restaurant where you can feel the true spirit of Japan
—a fusion of traditional Japanese techniques and the unique energy of Amsterdam—
not just through the flavors of the ramen,
but through the spotless space, attentive service,
and attention to every little detail.

Enjoy both our traditional meat- and seafood-based "ISM Series"
made with house-made noodles by chefs straight from Japan,
as well as our innovative 100% plant-based "Plant-Based Ramen"
that's fully vegan-friendly.

Whether you're sightseeing or taking a break from work
around the Lange Niezel area, please drop by.

Overview

ISM SERIES

Our original ramen series combines carefully crafted soups—such as shoyu or miso—prepared by skilled artisans with our house-made noodles.

PLANT-BASED RAMEN

A fully Plant-Based Ramen created in
collaboration with Amsterdam’s renowned
“MEN' IMPOSSIBLE.”

LUNCH SET

Our lunch set, available only 12:00–15:00,
is a great deal that includes ramen plus
gyoza or takoyaki and one drink.

HOUSE ORIGINALS

We also serve classic Japanese dishes like gyoza dumplings, edamame, and karaage fried chicken. Our vegan options are especially popular.

Always Aim Higher.
Never Compromise.

We believe that ramen is an evolving art, and we never stop pursuing the perfect bowl.
To ensure that we always serve the most delicious version of our craft,
we constantly evolve and change our recipes and menu over time.
Of course, you can order à la carte, but we also offer great-value sets tailored to lunch and dinner hours.
Pair your favorite ramen and snacks with Japanese beer or our original drinks.
Come enjoy a memorable experience at RAMEN-ISM,
where we always aim higher and approach everything with full passion.

Where people

We have a total of 31 seats: 17 on the 1st floor and 14 on the 2nd floor.

Whether you're dining solo, as a couple or with a group,

Feel free to reach out if you're planning a special gathering or party—we'd love to help make it memorable!
