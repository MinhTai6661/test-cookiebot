Delivery & Takeaway

  • At “RAMEN-ISM”, the authentic Japanese restaurant in the heart of Amsterdam,
    we’re happy to offer both delivery and takeaway so you can enjoy our ramen wherever you are.
    We are happy to offer a diverse menu featuring
    both Authentic Japanese Ramen and 100% Plant-Based options,
    ensuring a satisfying experience for everyone, from meat lovers to vegans and vegetarians.
  • To guarantee the perfect texture,
    our soup and noodles are packed separately in specialized containers so they never get soggy.
    They are ready to eat, but we recommend a gentle reheat at home—
    the flavors truly come alive when served warm.
  • To complete your meal, we also offer classic Japanese snacks
    such as our crispy Karaage (Japanese fried chicken) and Deep-fried Gyoza (dumplings).
    They are the perfect pairing for your ramen,
    whether it’s for a casual lunch, a relaxed dinner, or a special gathering.

How to Enjoy
Your Order

  • Delivery and takeaway orders can be placed through popular delivery platforms.
    Simply select your preferred pickup or delivery method, choose your menu items, and proceed to secure payment.
  • *For takeaway, you’re also very welcome to order directly at the restaurant in person.

Q&A

Q. How do I place a delivery order?
A. Please order through your preferred delivery platforms.
Q. How do I place a takeaway order?
A. You can order for pickup via delivery platforms,
or simply come by the restaurant and order in person.
Q. Are the prices the same as in the restaurant?
A. Delivery prices may vary. Please check each delivery platform for the latest pricing.
Q. What menu items can I order?
A. Please check the full available menu directly on each delivery platform.
Q. Do you have vegan or vegetarian options?
A. Yes. We offer 100% plant-based Plant-Based Ramen,
plus a selection of vegan- and vegetarian-friendly side dishes.
Q. Is there a minimum order quantity?
A. No restrictions at all—you’re welcome to order even just one item.
