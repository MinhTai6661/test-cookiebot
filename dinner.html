An Unwavering Bowl

At "RAMEN-ISM", the authentic Japanese restaurant in the heart of Amsterdam, you can enjoy genuine ramen even during dinner hours.

Our two flagship offerings are the meat- and seafood-based "ISM series" and our 100% plant-based "Plant-Based Ramen," both of which represent our never-ending pursuit of the perfect bowl.

The "ISM series" features classic Japanese ramen built around richly layered dashi broths—from fragrant shoyu to deep savory miso and boldly spiced tantan style.

Our "Plant-Based Ramen," supervised by the MEN' IMPOSSIBLE brand, is made with a full-bodied umami depth; it's loved by vegans, vegetarians, and ramen enthusiasts alike.

We also serve classic Japanese izakaya-style snacks like gyoza and takoyaki—perfect to pair with your ramen or drinks, such as sake.

Come relax and enjoy our latest evolved creations.

MENU

【ISM series】
TONKOTSU-ISM

Dive into our signature 18-hour slow-cooked pork broth, where creamy richness meets the deep umami of artisanal soy sauce.
A masterpiece of balance, topped with melt-in-your-mouth chashu and the smoky punch of house-made black garlic oil (Mayu).
This is more than a bowl—it’s a Japanese classic, perfected.

【ISM series】
MISO-ISM（TONKOTSU）

Dive into a deeply satisfying bowl where rich, slow-cooked tonkotsu broth meets the bold, savory depth of premium miso.The soup is thick, hearty, and packed with umami—perfectly clinging to our springy, curly noodles for a full-bodied experience in every bite.

Topped with tender braised pork and a generous serving of vegetables, this bowl delivers both richness and balance.Comforting yet powerful, it’s a true expression of Japanese soul food—warm, filling, and unforgettable.

【ISM series】
EBI-ISM

Our velvety, creamy soup is enriched with house-made roasted shrimp oil for a deep, aromatic finish.
Topped with succulent sweet-chili shrimp, fresh Mizuna greens, and crisp purple onions, this bowl offers a perfect harmony of richness and crunch.
Complete with a golden ajitama egg and shredded chili for a sophisticated touch.

【Plant-Based Ramen】
Creamy Umami

The flagship ramen from MEN' IMPOSSIBLE.
A concentrated, full-bodied broth drawn from various vegetables and dried ingredients,
enhanced with our original blended salt, and finished with intensely aromatic charred black garlic oil for unforgettable depth and fragrance.

【Plant-Based Ramen】
AMAZE MEN

Crafted with 100% plant-based ingredients and MEN' IMPOSSIBLE plant-based protein, this signature saucy ramen features our house-made noodles coated in a rich, creamy sauce full of depth and character.

Fragrant burnt scallion oil, crispy cauliflower tempura, marinated bamboo shoots, and sweet corn create a delightful contrast of textures and flavors in every bite.
